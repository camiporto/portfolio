About Me

My Story

Hi! I am Camilla, a UX/UI designer based in Salt Lake City, UT.

I was born in Brazil and moved quite often growing up, which greatly influenced how I see the world and my openness to art, culture and food. I love to cook and explore new places with friends and family. I like to use my experience with science, technology and education to enhance the way people interact with my designs. One of my favorite activities is to think about everyday problems creatively and produce new and more fun ways to do things.
